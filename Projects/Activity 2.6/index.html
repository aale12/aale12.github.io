Turtle Facts!

  1. 1. Turtles are theorized to first have appeared during the late Triassic Period.
  2. 2. Their shell may have evolved from primordial backbones and ribs.
  3. 3. Unfortunately, the top 25 species of turtles are likely to be extinct, and an inclusive total of 40 species are 'very likely' to become extinct.[1]
